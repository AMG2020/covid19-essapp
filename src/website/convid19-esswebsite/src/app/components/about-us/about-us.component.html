COVID-19 virtual Hackathon A virtual hackathon kicked off on the 18th March 2020 in Namibia for a COVID 19 tech response. Teams are developing:
      An essential info app for Namibia
      Realtime data tracking
      Virtual Learning
      Self reporting
      AI solution for health monitoring
      Misinformation and sentiment analysis
Virtual Hackathon for Namibia’s COVID-19 Tech Response has 75 local & international people across projects working around the clock with Min of Health/WHO/CDC.
